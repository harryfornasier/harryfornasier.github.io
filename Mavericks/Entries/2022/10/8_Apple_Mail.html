Apple Mail

 

Apple mail on mavericks should work out of the box with Icloud email, at least it did for mine. Gmail was a little bit trickier. You have to enable two factor authentication on your google account for it to work. After you’ve done this you can make an app specific password, which you’ll use to login to your email on Apple Mail, if done correctly it should load up everything without any issue.
